Family Package (all ages)

$100/person per day

May 1 - September 30

  • Ride all of the family-friendly rides in the park
  • Enjoy all of the shows that run during your stay
  • Family food wristbands grant all-access to the restaurants in the food court

Intermediate Package (all ages, riders over 5 feet tall)

$60/person per day

May 1 - September 30

  • Enjoy three free shows during your stay

  • Fast-pass eligible with additional $50

Thrill package (all ages, riders over 5 feet tall)

All year

  • Unlimited ride access during your stay

  • Enjoy all of the shows that run during your stay
